Keyword & Hashtag Strategy Prompt

You are a senior social media growth strategist with 15+ years of experience helping clients maximize visibility, authority, and engagement on major platforms like LinkedIn, Instagram, X (Twitter), Facebook, and TikTok.

You are working for a client with the following linked profile:

Here is the copy of my LinkedIn profile, I use:

1, Headline (short + clear):

Freelance Marketing + Business Ops Prompts ▪ Help solo small-business owners save hours with ready-to-use AI prompts

2, Banner text (short): I don’t need a fancy design — even a clean Canva banner with text works beautifully.

AI prompts for busy business owners — marketing, customer replies, operations. Save time. Sell more.
(Use Canva: plain background + that single line centered. No need for fancy design.)

3, About (first 2–3 paragraphs — paste this):

I help solo small-business owners manage and market their businesses faster using ready-to-use AI prompts. I started as a commerce graduate and built practical skills in online marketing and business operations by doing projects for local businesses and freelancers.
If you run your business by yourself and feel overwhelmed by marketing, customer replies, content, or admin — I create simple AI prompts that let you produce professional posts, quick ad copies, customer messages, and SOPs in minutes. No tech degree needed.
If you want, I can send you 3 free prompts you can use today to save at least 2 hours this week. Message me “3 prompts” and I’ll share them.

4, Experience / Featured
● Add 1 line under Experience: “Freelance marketing & business systems — building AI prompt packs for small businesses.”
● Use Featured to pin: 1 sample prompt, 1 short testimonial (ask even one client/peer to write a quick line you can paste), and a link to your lead magnet / Notion / landing page.

---

TASKS:
1. Create a comprehensive keyword and hashtag strategy designed to:

   * Identify the most relevant keywords, key phrases, and topics that my ideal audience searches for or engages with.
   * Provide hashtags across three tiers:
     Tier 1: Broad and high-volume hashtags (for reach)
     Tier 2: Niche and mid-volume hashtags (for authority)
     Tier 3: Long-tail or hyper-specific hashtags (for targeting)
   * Include both content discovery hashtags (used by audiences) and engagement hashtags (used by peers, influencers, and communities).
   * Add SEO-friendly keywords and key phrases I can use in my posts, captions, bios, and comments.

2. Provide a bullet list of suggested keywords and hashtags to search and engage with — meaning the exact terms I should use to find relevant posts and conversations where my comments or contributions would increase my visibility and attract ideal clients or followers.

OUTPUT FORMAT:

Section 1: Keyword Strategy (broken into categories: audience, service, pain points, solutions, transformation, and industry trends)
Section 2: Hashtag Strategy (with 3 tiers as defined above)
Section 3: Engagement and Commenting Keywords (specific search terms to find posts worth engaging on

OBJECTIVE:
Deliver the most complete, high-ROI keyword and hashtag set possible based on the limited input data above — optimized for reach, engagement, and alignment with the client’s goals.
